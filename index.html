School Week
Mon – Fri
Standard week — no Harlequins, no SIBN game
6:30

Wake Up

Glass of water first. Wash face, get dressed, eat something before leaving.

6:35

Feed Lincoln & Batman → How We Feel

Feed the cats — that's your anchor. While they eat, open How We Feel. 60-second check-in. Tap what you're feeling, move on. Day started.

6:40

Busuu — Spanish (5–10 min)

Before you leave the house. This is your streak. Protect it. 5 minutes is enough to keep it alive on busy mornings.

7:25

Bus → Globo

On the bus, open Globo. 15 minutes of passive learning instead of doom scrolling.

7:40

Arrive at SIHS

Use time before 8:20 to get organized, look over anything due that day.

8:20

Classes Begin

5 periods. Stay present, take notes. Easier to retain than cramming later.

MID-DAY

Formation — 40 Minutes

Use this. Knock out at least one assignment every single formation. This is your secret weapon for keeping evenings light.

3:05

School Ends — Work While You Wait

Mom gets off at 4:30 so you're waiting anyway. Use this time — knock out more homework or review notes. Anything you finish here means less to do tonight.

5:00

Home — Decompress + Feed Cats

30 min to eat, chill, reset. Feed Lincoln and Batman their evening meal. Don't let this stretch into the whole evening.

5:30

Homework Block

1–1.5 hours. You already knocked something out at formation — finish the rest now while your brain still works.

7:00

Workout (Evening)

20–30 min bodyweight session. Follow the push/pull/legs split in the Workouts section.

7:30

Shower + Skin Routine

Shower after workout, wash face, moisturize. Make it automatic.

8:00

Dishes

Rinse or wash whatever's from dinner. Takes 5–10 minutes. Do it now so it doesn't pile up overnight.

8:15

Free Time

Gaming, music, whatever. This is yours — you've earned it.

9:30

Wind Down — Podcast On

Phone face down. Put on Woe.Begone or whatever you're into. Dim the lights. No scrolling — story podcasts only from here.

9:45

Apple Journal — 2 Minutes

One or two sentences before sleep. What happened, how you feel, one thing for tomorrow. Close the loop on the day.

10:00

Lights Out

8+ hours sleep target. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep — this matters.

Formation Strategy — The Real Deal

Mon, Thu, Fri you have 40 min — that's enough for one solid assignment or two small ones. Use it every time, no exceptions. Wednesday's 15 min is just for quick stuff. Tuesday has nothing, so the evening block is your only shot. Plan accordingly.
